## üí° Tips for Creating Deliverables

### Quality Over Quantity
- Focus on creating portfolio-worthy pieces
- Each deliverable should demonstrate a specific skill
- Use real or realistic examples, not generic placeholders

### Make It Personal
- Choose products/industries you're interested in
- Apply frameworks to problems you've encountered
- Showcase your unique perspective and insights

### Document Your Thinking
- Explain not just **what** but **why**
- Show your decision-making process
- Include alternative approaches you considered

### Keep It Professional
- Use clear formatting and structure
- Proofread for typos and errors
- Include visual aids (diagrams, tables, charts)
- Make it something you'd share with hiring managers

## üìÅ Organization Tips

### Naming Convention
Use clear, consistent naming:
- `week-XX-deliverable-name.md`
- Include dates: `2025-01-15-product-roadmap.md`

### Version Control
- Track iterations of key deliverables
- Add v1, v2 suffixes for major revisions
- Keep a changelog in complex documents

### Link Related Work
- Cross-reference related deliverables
- Build on previous work
- Create a cohesive portfolio
