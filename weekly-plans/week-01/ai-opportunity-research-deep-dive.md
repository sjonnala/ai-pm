## ðŸ“Š Opportunity Scoring Model

Use a **4x4 matrix** to avoid pet-project bias.

| Dimension | Weight | Scoring Prompts (1-5) |
|-----------|--------|-----------------------|
| **User Value** | 30% | How drastically does this improve key workflow KPIs? |
| **Strategic Leverage** | 25% | Does it accelerate North Star metric or beachhead market? |
| **AI Feasibility** | 20% | Do we have/obtain the data + model capability in <3 months? |
| **Differentiation** | 15% | Does AI unlock something competitors cannot easily copy? |
| **Excitement / Narrative** | 10% | Will this story excite leadership & recruiting? |

Calculate weighted score, rank, and annotate assumptions + blockers beside each opportunity.

## ðŸ§  Opportunity Narrative (1-Pager)

Structure your pitch:

1. **Hook:** 2-sentence customer story with data proof.
2. **Problem & Impact:** SCO (Situation, Complication, Opportunity) framing.
3. **Vision Teaser:** Imagine-if statement that highlights the AI superpower.
4. **Proof of Feasibility:** Data availability, model strategy, guardrails.
5. **Next Steps:** Research gaps, stakeholders, and pilot timeline.

Bring this to stakeholder reviews to secure alignment and resources quickly.
