## 8. Risks & Mitigations

### User Adoption Risks

**Risk:** Users don't trust/use AI features
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Start with augmentation, not replacement
  - Show confidence/transparency
  - Easy opt-out
  - Gradual rollout with feedback loops

### Technical Risks

**Risk:** AI quality doesn't meet expectations
- **Likelihood:** Medium-High
- **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Start with well-scoped use cases
  - Set clear success thresholds
  - Have fallback mechanisms
  - Continuous monitoring

### Competitive Risks

**Risk:** Competitors move faster
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Move quickly on crawl phase
  - Build defensible walk/run initiatives
  - Focus on unique data/insights

### Cost Risks

**Risk:** AI costs are unsustainable
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Model cost projections
  - Optimize inference costs
  - Consider usage-based pricing
  - Plan for economies of scale
